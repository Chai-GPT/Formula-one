03
Engineering The Speed

The car is a 1000+ HP hybrid. The magic is how it uses a tiny 1.6-Liter V6 (like in a family car) to produce this power.

>52%
The World's Most Efficient Engine
  • >ICE (Engine): The 1.6L V6 spins to 15,000 RPM (a road car is ~6,000).
  • >MGU-K (Kinetic): Recovers energy from braking for a 160hp boost.
  • >MGU-H (Heat): Recovers waste energy from the turbocharger to eliminate "turbo lag" and charge the battery.
  • >AERO: The wings & floor create 3,000kg of downforce, enough to drive upside down at 180 mph.
04
The Profitability

Prize money covers only a fraction of the true cost. F1 teams don’t operate for profit — the sport is a global marketing expense. Teams rely on owners to cover the deficit.
